About

What Nightmare Logistics Is About

Nightmare Logistics is an homage to old cars that smell like gasoline, rattle at idle, and still deserve another year on the road. No staged “build reveals”—just real trips in real cars that might or might not make it.

  • Buy classics and antiques at auctions and drive them home. No trailer, no backup plan.
  • Document the reality of old-car travel: breakdowns, roadside fixes, late night diners, and questionable motels.
  • Share honest reviews of how these cars actually live on the highway—decades after they left the factory.
  • Bring other owners along for the ride with guest cars, feature runs, and collabs.
  • Find you a car, drive it home for you, creating and posting videos of your car and the adventure
